## PrimeBiometry Assessment

eFACiLiTY Time and Attendance System is a module within Sierra Technologies' broader facilities management platform, in operation since 1997. It addresses a specific operational problem: eliminating manual attendance disputes by pulling real-time data from biometric devices, access control systems, and mobile apps into a unified workforce management layer. This is not a pure identity verification play -- it is a workforce automation tool where biometric authentication (fingerprint, facial recognition) is the data capture mechanism, not the core product. Early-stage vendor with limited published reviews.

### Best For

Mid-to-large enterprises in manufacturing, construction, healthcare, or facilities-heavy industries that are already running Sierra's eFACiLiTY suite and need biometric attendance integrated with access control, shift rostering, and overtime calculation -- particularly in India and Southeast Asia where Sierra has established presence.

### Avoid If

Organizations looking for a standalone identity verification or KYC product -- this is workforce time-attendance software. Also avoid if your workforce headcount makes the $1,200/user/month pricing model economically unrealistic; this price point implies enterprise licensing, not per-seat SaaS.

### Integration Complexity

**High** — eFACiLiTY is a facilities management platform, not an API-first product. Integration with third-party biometric hardware (readers, clocks), access control panels, and HR/payroll systems requires Sierra's implementation team. Expect project-based deployment with weeks to months of setup time, not self-serve onboarding.

### Pricing Analysis

The published price of $1,200/user/month is one of the highest per-user figures in this vendor set. At face value, this figure is likely a list price for a fully bundled enterprise deployment rather than a true per-seat monthly fee -- Sierra's model is closer to traditional enterprise software licensing than SaaS. Verify whether this is per-user annual licensing, per-device, or a platform fee during initial sales contact.

For organizations with 500+ employees and existing eFACiLiTY infrastructure, the pricing may be more favorable when negotiated as part of a broader Sierra suite deal. For greenfield deployments, compare the total cost of ownership against purpose-built time-attendance platforms with biometric integration before committing to this ecosystem.

## FAQ

**Is eFACiLiTY Time and Attendance a standalone product or part of a larger suite?**
It is a module within Sierra Technologies' eFACiLiTY Integrated Workplace Management System (IWMS). It is most effective when deployed alongside other eFACiLiTY modules like access control, visitor management, and facility booking.

**What biometric hardware does eFACiLiTY support?**
The system integrates with a range of third-party biometric devices including fingerprint scanners, facial recognition terminals, and card readers. Specific hardware compatibility lists should be requested from Sierra Technologies' implementation team.

**Does eFACiLiTY support mobile-based attendance capture?**
Yes. In addition to fixed biometric devices, the system supports mobile app-based attendance capture, which is relevant for field workers, remote sites, or distributed teams.

**What industries is eFACiLiTY Time and Attendance primarily deployed in?**
Sierra Technologies targets facilities-intensive sectors including manufacturing, healthcare, real estate, oil and gas, and government. The platform has significant deployments in India and the Middle East.

**Is there a free trial available?**
No free trial is publicly listed. Evaluation typically requires engagement with Sierra Technologies' sales and pre-sales team for a demonstration and scoped deployment proposal.
