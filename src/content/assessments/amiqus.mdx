## PrimeBiometry Assessment

Amiqus is a UK-centric compliance onboarding platform built for regulated professional services firms — law firms, recruitment agencies, financial advisors, and healthcare employers — that need to simultaneously manage client KYC, staff right-to-work verification, DBS criminal record checks, and AML screening from a single platform. This dual client-and-staff onboarding architecture is unusual and directly addresses a genuine operational pain point for UK regulated sectors where the same compliance team handles both employee vetting and client due diligence. Amiqus is among the most transparently priced compliance platforms available — the two-track pricing (candidate checks vs client onboarding) is published openly and current per-check rates are shown in the sidebar.

## Pricing Analysis

Amiqus publishes genuinely detailed pricing, which is rare in the compliance platform category. The two-track structure — candidate checks on one side, client onboarding on the other — is unusual: organizations needing both effectively run two pricing tracks simultaneously and should model each separately when comparing total cost against generalist KYC vendors.

The Enterprise tier for both tracks is custom-priced and includes data hosting localization, dedicated implementation management, and SLAs — the relevant upgrade point for firms with higher annual throughput or complex HRMS/practice management integration requirements. For firms evaluating return on investment, the measurable comparison point is manual right-to-work and AML check processing costs (typically 20-60 minutes per case including chasing) versus Amiqus's automated workflow.

## FAQ

**What UK-specific compliance checks does Amiqus support that generic KYC platforms do not?**
Amiqus supports Right to Work checks (UK Home Office compliant), BPSS (Baseline Personnel Security Standard) screening, Digital DBS (Disclosure and Barring Service) checks, and automated employment referencing — checks that are legally required in many UK employment contexts and are not typically available from non-UK KYC vendors.

**Can Amiqus handle both clients and candidates in the same organization account?**
Yes. The platform explicitly serves dual use cases: client onboarding (KYC/AML for regulated professional services) and staff/candidate checks (right to work, DBS, referencing). A single Amiqus subscription covers both use cases, which is the platform's core differentiation from either a pure-KYC or pure-HR-screening tool.

**Is there a Digital Wallet feature that benefits repeat clients?**
Amiqus includes a Digital Wallet for reused documents, allowing clients who have previously verified their identity with an Amiqus-using firm to share their verified credentials with another Amiqus firm without repeating the verification process. This is a meaningful friction reducer in the legal and financial advisory sectors where clients often have multiple professional service relationships.

**What data residency options are available for UK-based organizations post-Brexit?**
The Enterprise tier explicitly includes "data hosting localisation" as a feature, indicating UK-based data storage is available for organizations that need to maintain data within UK jurisdiction under UK GDPR requirements. Standard tier organizations should confirm their data residency arrangement during contract negotiation.

**How does Amiqus pricing compare to hiring a compliance officer for manual checks?**
For small to mid-size firms, manual processing is often more expensive than Amiqus licensing once staff time, error risk, and audit trail requirements are costed. The Scale tier with API automation is typically the threshold at which Amiqus becomes cheaper than manual workflows combined with document storage overhead.
