## PrimeBiometry Assessment

SwiftDil is a London-based SaaS and API platform that positions itself explicitly as a single-integration solution covering identity verification, AML screening, and KYC compliance — the "all obligations through one API" pitch targets regulated businesses that currently maintain multiple vendor relationships for each compliance function. This approach is differentiated from pure biometric verification vendors by including AML and watchlist screening as first-class features within the same API rather than as add-ons. With 2 G2 reviews averaging 4.3, it has modest but positive independent validation.

### Best For

Regulated businesses in the UK and EU (fintechs, payment institutions, crypto exchanges, legal firms) operating under FCA, PSD2, or 5AMLD/6AMLD obligations that want to consolidate AML screening, biometric identity verification, and document authentication into a single API integration and billing relationship.

### Avoid If

Organizations based primarily in the US, Asia, or other regions where UK/EU AML regulatory frameworks are not the primary compliance driver. SwiftDil's AML database coverage and regulatory alignment are optimized for UK and EU requirements. Also avoid if your team needs a no-code dashboard for non-technical compliance officers — SwiftDil is built for developer-led integration.

### Integration Complexity

**Low** — REST API, Web SDK, iOS SDK, and Android SDK are all available. The platform also lists pre-built connectors for Salesforce, Shopify, and Zapier, expanding accessibility for non-developer users. Cloud-based deployment is default with on-premises options available.

### Pricing Analysis

SwiftDil's listed tiers (Basic $29/month for 100 verifications, Pro $79/month for 500 verifications, Enterprise custom) appear to be scraped estimates; the official site shows "Contact vendor" pricing. Given the combined AML + KYC offering, effective cost-per-verification needs to be evaluated against vendors that charge separately for each service.

The pricing model is best evaluated on a total compliance stack basis: if you are currently paying separately for an AML screening service and a KYC verification service, consolidating both into SwiftDil may produce cost savings even if the headline per-verification rate appears higher than a standalone KYC tool. For high-volume buyers (10,000+ monthly checks), negotiate a custom volume rate rather than scaling on published tiers.

## FAQ

**Does SwiftDil's AML screening cover global sanctions lists or only UK/EU watchlists?**
SwiftDil offers AML screening as part of its platform, but buyers should explicitly confirm the geographic scope of watchlist coverage — specifically whether it includes OFAC (US), UN consolidated lists, EU consolidated lists, and jurisdiction-specific PEP databases relevant to their customer base.

**How does SwiftDil handle ongoing monitoring versus point-in-time AML screening?**
The platform is described as covering ongoing AML/KYC obligations, suggesting it supports re-screening against updated watchlists. Buyers should confirm the re-screening frequency (real-time, daily, weekly) and whether alerts are surfaced in a dashboard or pushed via webhook.

**What biometric capabilities does SwiftDil include — liveness detection, document OCR, or both?**
SwiftDil includes biometric matching, facial recognition, liveness detection, and document scanning as listed features. Buyers requiring certified liveness (iBeta Level 2) should confirm the certification status of the liveness component specifically, as not all vendors that list "liveness detection" hold formal anti-spoofing certifications.

**Is SwiftDil suitable for crypto exchange KYC compliance under UK FCA registration?**
Crypto exchanges registered with the FCA under MLR 2017 need both identity verification and AML screening for all customers. SwiftDil's single-API approach covering both obligations is directly relevant to this use case, though buyers should confirm coverage for high-risk jurisdictions and enhanced due diligence workflows.

**Does SwiftDil offer a KYB (Know Your Business) verification capability?**
KYB is not explicitly listed in SwiftDil's feature set. Regulated businesses that need to verify both individual customers and corporate entities (directors, UBOs, shareholders) should confirm whether corporate verification is available or whether a separate vendor is needed for KYB.
