## PrimeBiometry Assessment

Jumio is one of the most established names in automated identity verification, founded in 2010 and backed by $150M in private equity funding. Its platform spans the full verification stack: government ID authentication, facial biometrics with liveness detection, and integrated AML screening — all within a single vendor relationship. This breadth is Jumio's primary differentiator against point solutions; it eliminates the need to stitch together separate KYC, biometric, and AML vendors. Integration uses Web SDK, iOS SDK, Android SDK, or REST API, with a publicly accessible developer portal; expect 1–2 weeks for a web onboarding flow and an additional 1–2 weeks for native mobile SDK work. However, with zero published reviews on major platforms despite its market prominence, buyers should conduct reference checks directly with Jumio's customer success team before contracting.

## Pricing Analysis

Jumio's published pricing tiers (Starter at $0, Pro at $199/month) appear to be entry-level tiers with limited volume. In practice, Jumio's per-verification pricing for meaningful volumes is negotiated via enterprise contract. The $199/month "Pro" tier is unlikely to accommodate production workloads for most B2B buyers.

For organizations running 10,000+ verifications/month, expect a custom per-verification rate with volume discounts and a minimum annual commitment. AML screening adds cost per check on top of the base verification fee. Budget for a total cost well above the published tier prices. When negotiating, push for a dedicated success manager, SLA for verification turnaround time, and data residency options upfront — these are easier to secure at contract time than as add-ons.

## FAQ

**Does Jumio cover both ID document verification and biometric matching?**
Yes. Jumio's platform includes government-issued ID authentication (passports, driver's licenses, national IDs across 200+ countries), biometric facial comparison with liveness detection, and AML screening — all accessible via a single API integration.

**What liveness detection standard does Jumio's biometric system meet?**
Jumio uses AI-powered liveness detection and claims compliance with ISO 30107-3 passive liveness standards. Buyers with high fraud risk (crypto, gaming) should verify the specific iBeta PAD Level tested against current standards.

**Is Jumio suitable for GDPR-regulated customer data?**
Yes. Jumio holds GDPR compliance and supports data processing agreements. Data residency options (EU, US) are available for enterprise contracts — confirm the default residency in your contract.

**How does Jumio handle verification failures and manual review?**
Jumio operates an automated review pipeline with fallback to human review for edge cases. Manual review SLAs and the ratio of automated vs. human decisions should be discussed during the sales process, as they directly affect your onboarding conversion rate.

**What is the minimum viable contract size for Jumio?**
Jumio does not publish minimum contract terms. Based on market positioning and funding stage, expect minimum annual commitments in the range of $12,000-$50,000 for mid-tier accounts. Startups below this threshold should evaluate Starter-tier or contact the sales team about growth programs.
