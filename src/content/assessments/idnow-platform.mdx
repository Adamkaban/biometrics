## PrimeBiometry Assessment

IDnow is a Munich-based identity platform with a distinct specialty: video-assisted identity verification (Video-Ident) that meets German BaFin and EU eIDAS regulatory requirements — a niche where few international vendors can match its compliance depth. Founded in 2014 and acquired (indicating stability under a larger parent), IDnow has built its product around the stringent requirements of DACH-region financial institutions, where human-assisted verification and qualified electronic signatures are often mandated. Its 95% identification accuracy claim and ISO 27001 certification position it for regulated enterprise buyers rather than high-volume automated onboarding.

### Best For

EU-regulated financial institutions — particularly banks, fintechs, and insurance companies in Germany, Austria, and Switzerland — that need a single vendor covering both automated KYC and video-assisted identity verification with legally binding eIDAS-compliant digital signatures.

### Avoid If

Companies primarily operating outside the EU, or those requiring fully automated document verification at high volume with per-verification pricing. IDnow's enterprise-only pricing and DACH focus make it inefficient for global consumer-scale use cases.

### Integration Complexity

**Low** — REST API, Mobile SDK (iOS/Android), and Web SDK are all available. Standard integration typically completes in 2-4 weeks. Video-Ident workflows require additional QA given the human-review component.

### Pricing Analysis

IDnow operates on a contact-for-pricing enterprise model across all tiers (Basic, Professional, Enterprise). There are no published rates, which is standard for the DACH regulated-sector market where pricing is typically volume-based and contract-length-dependent.

Buyers should expect minimum annual contract commitments, with pricing that reflects both automated verification volume and Video-Ident session costs (human agents are more expensive per transaction). For organizations whose regulatory requirements mandate Video-Ident, IDnow is one of the few qualified vendors — negotiate on annual verification volume and SLA uptime, especially for the digital signature component which may be priced separately.

## FAQ

**What is Video-Ident and why does it matter for German financial regulation?**
Video-Ident is a live video call between a customer and a trained IDnow agent who verifies identity documents in real time. German BaFin regulations and some EU AML directives require this method for high-value financial product onboarding where fully automated verification is not legally sufficient.

**Does IDnow support digital signatures compliant with eIDAS?**
Yes. IDnow eSign provides qualified electronic signatures (QES) that are legally binding across the EU under eIDAS regulation. This is a differentiating capability for regulated industries needing contract execution and identity verification in the same workflow.

**Is IDnow available outside Germany and the EU?**
IDnow primarily serves Germany, Austria, Switzerland, and other EU countries. While technically accessible elsewhere, document coverage and support quality are strongest in the DACH region.

**How does IDnow's acquisition status affect buyers?**
IDnow has been acquired, which provides financial stability but may affect product roadmap independence. Buyers should clarify the acquiring entity and any changes to support or pricing during contract negotiations.

**What is IDnow's PCI DSS certification relevant for?**
PCI DSS compliance means IDnow meets payment card industry data security standards, relevant if the identity verification workflow intersects with payment processing or financial onboarding.
