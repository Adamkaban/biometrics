## FAQ

**What is AU10TIX's Serial Fraud Monitor and how does it work?**
Serial Fraud Monitor cross-references biometric and document data from new verification attempts against AU10TIX's consortium database of previously identified fraudulent identities. This means that a fraudster who was caught attempting fraud on one AU10TIX customer is flagged across all customers using the feature — without exposing the underlying identity data. It is particularly effective against organized fraud rings using synthetic or stolen identities at scale.

**How does AU10TIX's deepfake detection work?**
AU10TIX's deepfake detection analyzes video and image artifacts that indicate AI-generated or manipulated facial images, including digital injection attacks where pre-recorded or synthetic video is injected into the camera stream. This capability is distinct from passive liveness detection and addresses the emerging threat of AI-generated identity fraud.

**What gaming regulatory certifications does AU10TIX hold?**
AU10TIX holds PGCB (Pennsylvania Gaming Control Board) and NGCB (Nevada Gaming Control Board) certifications, making it one of the few IDV vendors with verified approval from major US gaming regulators. This is a hard requirement for online gambling platforms in these jurisdictions.

**Does AU10TIX support reusable digital identity credentials?**
Yes. The Reusable Digital ID product allows a user who has been verified once to reuse tamper-proof digital credentials across multiple platforms that use AU10TIX. This reduces friction for repeat onboarding and is relevant for platforms participating in the digital credential ecosystem.

**What is AU10TIX's document coverage compared to competitors?**
AU10TIX supports 5,000+ document types with a claimed 99% pass rate and 90% reduction in unidentified documents. This is comparable to Veriff (230+ countries) and Sumsub, and is particularly strong for physical document formats encountered in gaming and hospitality where diverse ID types are common.
