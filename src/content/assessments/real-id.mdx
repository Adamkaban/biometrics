## PrimeBiometry Assessment

Real ID is Zoloz's identity verification product, backed by Ant Group's financial-grade infrastructure and built for high-volume KYC automation in Asian and emerging markets. The solution pairs document authentication with facial recognition, making it particularly relevant for financial institutions and fintechs that need reliable verification across a broad range of national ID formats. With only two published reviews on Gartner (rating 4.5/5), independently verified performance data is sparse, which is a meaningful caveat for procurement teams.

### Best For

Financial institutions and fintechs operating in APAC markets that require document + biometric KYC automation at scale, where Ant Group's regional infrastructure and ID document coverage provide a practical advantage over Western-origin vendors.

### Avoid If

Teams that need a sandbox or free trial to evaluate accuracy before contracting — Real ID has no published trial option, and pricing is contact-only with no disclosed tiers.

### Integration Complexity

**Medium** — Zoloz provides an SDK and REST API for Real ID, but public documentation is limited and onboarding typically requires direct engagement with their sales team. Integration timelines will depend heavily on regional support availability.

### Pricing Analysis

Pricing is entirely contact-based with no published tiers. For organizations processing moderate verification volumes, this means a negotiated per-verification or volume-license structure. Expect that enterprise-grade pricing will be the default entry point, making Real ID a poor fit for early-stage startups or pilots under 10,000 verifications/month.

For larger deployments, Zoloz's Ant Group backing suggests capacity for competitive volume pricing, but only organizations with sufficient deal leverage will realize this. Request a formal commercial proposal early — do not assume favorable startup-tier terms.

## FAQ

**Who owns Real ID and what is the relationship to Zoloz?**
Real ID is the identity verification product within Zoloz, a company spun out of Ant Group (Alibaba's financial affiliate). Zoloz was established to commercialize Ant's internal identity infrastructure for external markets.

**What types of ID documents does Real ID support?**
As a Zoloz product, Real ID is designed with particular depth for APAC-region documents, including national ID cards, passports, and driver's licenses from countries across Southeast Asia and China. Coverage outside these regions should be verified directly with the vendor.

**Does Real ID offer a free trial or sandbox environment?**
No. Based on current public information, Real ID does not offer a free trial. Evaluation requires direct contact with the Zoloz sales team.

**Is Real ID suitable for GDPR-regulated deployments in the EU?**
Real ID's compliance certifications are not publicly documented. Organizations with EU regulatory obligations should conduct formal due diligence on data residency and GDPR compliance posture before procurement.

**How mature is Real ID compared to established Western IDV vendors?**
With only two published Gartner reviews and limited publicly available case studies, Real ID is significantly less validated in third-party review channels than vendors like Jumio or Onfido. This does not necessarily reflect product quality, but procurement teams should weight the lack of review volume accordingly.
