## PrimeBiometry Assessment

Biometric Attendance System by Cuckoo Tech is a narrowly scoped HR attendance product, not a general-purpose identity verification platform. It occupies a distinct niche: tracking employee presence using biometric signals (fingerprint/face) with geofencing for field workers, then feeding that data into payroll. For buyers shopping for KYC, fraud prevention, or customer onboarding tools, this vendor is categorically out of scope.

### Best For

Indian SMBs and mid-market companies with distributed or field-based workforces who need attendance compliance, leave management, and payroll integration in a single per-user SaaS — particularly those already running HR operations in the $29–49/user/month range.

### Avoid If

Any use case involving customer identity verification, AML screening, KYC compliance, or biometric authentication for end-user onboarding. This vendor has only 2 published reviews on Capterra, which limits independent validation of its claims.

### Integration Complexity

**Low** — REST API available with payroll system connectors; geofencing and mobile attendance suggest iOS/Android app coverage. Typical HR SaaS setup time is under a week for standard configurations.

### Pricing Analysis

At $29/user/month (Standard) and $49/user/month (Professional), Cuckoo Tech prices like a typical SMB HR SaaS product. For a 100-person company, that's $2,900–4,900/month — reasonable for combined attendance + leave + contractor management. Enterprise pricing is custom, which is standard for organizations above a few hundred seats.

The key question for buyers is whether payroll integration covers their specific system (e.g., SAP, Zoho Payroll, Keka). The vendor's India-first positioning suggests strongest native integrations with Indian HR platforms. Budget-conscious teams should evaluate whether the $29 Standard plan covers all needed geofencing features before committing to Professional.

## FAQ

**Is this product suitable for customer identity verification or KYC compliance?**
No. Biometric Attendance System is an HR workforce management tool. It tracks employee attendance using biometrics and geofencing. It does not support customer onboarding, document verification, or regulatory KYC/AML workflows.

**What biometric modalities does the system support?**
The platform supports biometric-based attendance tracking with geofencing for field employees. Specific modality details (fingerprint vs. facial recognition) should be confirmed directly with Cuckoo Tech, as the public documentation is limited.

**How many reviews does this vendor have?**
Only 2 published reviews on Capterra as of 2026. This is an early-stage or niche-market vendor with limited independent review coverage. Due diligence should include reference calls with existing customers.

**Does it integrate with Indian payroll systems?**
The vendor is headquartered in India and targets Indian SMBs, suggesting alignment with local HR and payroll platforms. Confirm specific integrations (Zoho Payroll, Keka, Darwinbox) during evaluation.

**What is the free trial scope?**
A free trial is available at cuckootech.com. Scope (number of users, duration, feature restrictions) should be confirmed with the vendor before committing to a paid tier.
