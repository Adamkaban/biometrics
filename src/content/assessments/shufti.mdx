## PrimeBiometry Assessment

Shufti (operating as Shufti Pro) is a full-stack KYC/AML platform that combines identity verification, AML screening, and compliance workflow tooling in a single product. Its standout characteristic is pricing accessibility: unlike most enterprise identity verification vendors, Shufti publishes a "Free Forever" tier (10 verifications/month, no card required) and an Essentials tier at $0.95/check for up to 20,000 verifications — making it one of the few vendors with genuinely transparent, developer-accessible pricing. Founded in 2016-2017 with $20M in Series A funding and a team of 201-500 employees, it serves the broadest industry coverage of any vendor in this batch, including gig economy and social network verticals that most KYC vendors ignore.

### Best For

Fintechs, crypto exchanges, forex platforms, and marketplace operators that need comprehensive KYC/AML with global coverage across 200+ countries and flexible pricing that scales from early-stage (free tier) through high-volume (enterprise custom) — particularly where the No-Code Journey Builder reduces engineering resource requirements for workflow customization.

### Avoid If

Organizations in healthcare or any regulated sector explicitly requiring HIPAA compliance — Shufti does not list HIPAA among its certifications, which disqualifies it for healthcare identity use cases where HIPAA is a procurement prerequisite.

### Integration Complexity

**Low** — REST API, Mobile SDK (iOS/Android), Web SDK, and on-premise deployment options are all available. The No-Code Journey Builder is a significant differentiator for non-engineering teams that need to modify verification workflows without developer involvement. Multiple deployment options (cloud and on-premise) add deployment flexibility.

### Pricing Analysis

Shufti's pricing structure is the most developer-friendly of any vendor in this comparison. The Free Forever tier (10 verifications/month, no card required) is a genuine free evaluation path. The Essentials tier at $0.95/check supports up to 20,000 verifications/month, making per-unit cost predictable for startups and growth-stage companies.

At 20,000+ verifications/month, buyers transition to the Enterprise tier with custom pricing. For high-volume users, the per-check rate will drop meaningfully from the Essentials $0.95 — negotiation should target the per-check rate, volume commit, and whether AML screening is bundled or billed separately. Buyers should also clarify whether NFC verification, Video Ident, and enhanced due diligence are included in base tiers or priced as add-ons.

## FAQ

**What AML screening databases does Shufti cover?**
Shufti supports real-time AML screening with access to global databases covering sanctions lists, politically exposed persons (PEPs), and adverse media. Buyers in regulated financial services should request the specific list sources and update frequency as part of due diligence, since AML screening quality depends heavily on database coverage depth.

**Does the $0.95/check Essentials tier include both document verification and biometric verification?**
The Essentials tier pricing is for individual service checks — document verification, biometric face verification, and AML screening may each be billed separately. Buyers should clarify whether a single KYC workflow that combines document + biometric + AML counts as one check or three before projecting costs.

**What does "No-Code Journey Builder" enable in practice?**
The No-Code Journey Builder allows compliance teams to configure verification workflows — which checks to run, in which order, with what acceptance thresholds — without requiring engineering involvement. This is valuable for organizations that need to rapidly adjust KYC flows in response to regulatory changes or fraud pattern shifts.

**Is on-premise deployment available on all pricing tiers, or only enterprise?**
On-premise deployment is listed as an option, but it is typically restricted to enterprise custom agreements. Buyers with data residency requirements should confirm on-premise availability and any associated premium pricing during the sales process.

**How does Shufti handle NFC chip verification for ePassports?**
NFC chip verification is listed as a capability in the Standard Plan and above, allowing direct reading of the chip embedded in ICAO-compliant ePassports for a higher level of document authenticity assurance compared to optical-only document checking. This is relevant for financial institutions with elevated fraud risk that need to go beyond visual document scanning.
