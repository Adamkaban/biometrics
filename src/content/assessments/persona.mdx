## PrimeBiometry Assessment

Persona stands out as a workflow-first identity platform rather than a point solution — its core value is a no-code/low-code flow builder that lets compliance and product teams reconfigure KYC, KYB, AML, and fraud prevention logic without engineering changes. Backed by $200M in Series D funding and covering 200+ countries, it targets product-led growth companies that need verification at scale. The platform's configurability is genuinely rare: most competitors require professional services to restructure verification sequences, while Persona exposes those controls directly.

### Best For

Fintech startups and mid-market marketplaces that need to run KYC, KYB, and fraud prevention from a single orchestration layer — especially teams that iterate on onboarding flows frequently or serve diverse geographic markets.

### Avoid If

Teams that only need basic document + selfie verification without multi-step orchestration. The platform's depth adds overhead for simple use cases, and enterprise-level pricing reflects that complexity.

### Integration Complexity

**Low** — REST API, Web SDK, and Mobile SDK for iOS/Android are all publicly available. Self-serve signup at withpersona.com; most teams complete sandbox integration within a week. On-premise deployment available for enterprise contracts.

### Pricing Analysis

Persona uses custom/enterprise pricing with no published tiers. The self-serve free trial covers sandbox access, but production credentials require a sales conversation. Startups and early-stage companies should expect pricing anchored to verification volume with a minimum commitment; sub-1,000 verifications/month rarely makes economic sense.

Mid-market buyers (10K–500K verifications/month) get the most value from Persona's orchestration layer — the cost per verification drops as volume increases, and workflow flexibility reduces engineering overhead. Enterprise buyers operating in regulated markets should negotiate data residency and SLA guarantees early, as these add cost but are non-negotiable for banking/healthcare use cases.

## FAQ

**Does Persona support Know Your Business (KYB) in addition to individual KYC?**
Yes. Persona includes KYB flows for business entity verification, beneficial ownership checks, and document collection — it can run KYC on individual directors within the same session as a KYB flow.

**What countries does Persona support for document verification?**
Persona supports documents from 200+ countries and territories. Coverage depth varies; major markets (US, EU, UK, APAC) have the highest acceptance rates and most document types supported.

**Can compliance teams change verification flows without engineering involvement?**
Yes — Persona's visual flow builder is designed for non-technical stakeholders. Compliance teams can add, remove, or reorder verification steps (document check, selfie, database lookup, manual review) through the dashboard.

**How does Persona handle manual review queues?**
Persona includes a built-in case management interface where flagged verifications route to human reviewers. Teams can add internal notes, request additional documents, and approve or reject cases directly from the dashboard.

**Is Persona suitable for high-risk use cases like crypto exchanges or gambling platforms?**
Yes. Persona is used in crypto, gaming, and marketplace verticals. For crypto specifically, it supports travel rule compliance and enhanced due diligence flows, though you should confirm AML list screening depth during the sales process.
