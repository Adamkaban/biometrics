### Pricing Analysis

ComplyCube's published tiers start at $99/month (Starter) and $299/month (Core), making entry-level access affordable for early-stage companies running volume below a few hundred verifications per month. For companies doing 1,000+ verifications/month, the per-verification economics at Enterprise will typically beat the flat monthly minimums. Buyers should push for volume-tiered pricing during negotiations, particularly if combining IDV and AML screening into a single contract — bundled pricing is where ComplyCube's competitive advantage concentrates relative to point-solution providers.

## FAQ

**What makes ComplyCube's NFC verification different from standard document scanning?**
NFC chip reading extracts biometric data directly from the secure chip in ePassports and national ID cards, verifying that the document's chip data matches the physical document and the person presenting it. This provides tamper-proof verification that optical scanning alone cannot achieve, and it satisfies higher-assurance requirements under UK DiATF and similar frameworks.

**Is ComplyCube certified for biometric liveness detection?**
Yes. ComplyCube holds iBeta PAD (Presentation Attack Detection) certification, confirming that its liveness detection meets ISO 30107-3 standards. This is a hard requirement for UK DiATF identity service provider status.

**Does ComplyCube support KYB (Know Your Business) in addition to KYC?**
Yes. Business verification is available through the platform, covering UBO identification, company registry checks, and sanctions screening for legal entities — relevant for B2B onboarding use cases in financial services.

**What is the difference between the Core and Growth tiers?**
Core ($299/mo) adds compliance policy templates, fraud intelligence, proof of address, and batch processing. Growth adds eID authentication, NFC chip verification, database checks, and custom pricing — it is the minimum tier for high-assurance identity verification in regulated markets.

**How does ComplyCube handle GDPR data subject deletion requests?**
ComplyCube provides API-level session deletion and data management workflows. As a UK-registered entity with ISO 27001 certification, it offers data processing agreements (DPAs) for EU/UK customers. Confirm data residency options (UK vs. EU data centers) during procurement if your compliance team requires specific regional data storage.
