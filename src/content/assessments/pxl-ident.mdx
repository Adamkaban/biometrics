## PrimeBiometry Assessment

PXL Ident is PXL Vision's identity verification product, built from the ground up for regulated European industries where compliance and data sovereignty are non-negotiable. The browser-based delivery model is a deliberate design choice: it avoids the app-store distribution friction that plagues native mobile KYC flows while maintaining biometric accuracy through in-browser AI processing. PXL Vision is a Seed-stage company (founded 2012) with no published reviews on major platforms, so production track record must be established through direct reference calls.

### Best For

European financial institutions, insurance companies, and regulated platforms that require identity verification meeting local regulatory standards (including Swiss FINMA-adjacent requirements). The browser-first approach suits onboarding flows where mobile app installation is not a realistic expectation.

### Avoid If

US-headquartered companies scaling beyond Europe, or teams that need native iOS/Android SDK deployment with offline capability. The absence of published reviews and opaque pricing make it a risky primary vendor choice without a validated enterprise reference.

## FAQ

**What makes PXL Ident different from larger identity verification vendors?**
PXL Ident is built specifically for European regulated industries with a browser-first delivery model and strong emphasis on Swiss data security standards. Larger vendors like Veriff and Jumio offer broader global coverage but may carry more compliance complexity for EU-focused operations.

**Does PXL Ident support mobile devices?**
Yes — the browser-based approach works on mobile browsers, but there is no dedicated native app or SDK for iOS/Android. Organizations requiring native mobile integration should evaluate this limitation against their user experience requirements.

**Is PXL Ident GDPR compliant?**
PXL Vision is a Swiss company with data handling practices aligned to European data protection standards. The product is designed for GDPR-covered markets, though buyers should request the Data Processing Agreement (DPA) to verify specifics.

**What industries does PXL Vision primarily serve?**
PXL Vision targets regulated industries including banking, insurance, fintech, and mobility services — particularly in the European market where identity verification regulation is strictest.

**How do I get pricing for PXL Ident?**
The listed €1/year Starter price is not indicative of production costs. Contact PXL Vision directly for volume-based pricing. Given the Seed funding stage, expect flexibility on pilot pricing for qualified enterprise opportunities.
