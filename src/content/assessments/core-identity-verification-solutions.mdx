## PrimeBiometry Assessment

CORE Identity Verification Solutions is the G2 product listing for Veridas, a Spanish AI biometrics company founded in 2019 with over $1B in total funding (acquired stage). Veridas distinguishes itself through a multi-modal biometric approach that combines facial recognition, voice authentication, and document verification in a single platform — a combination that goes beyond the document-plus-selfie model most competitors offer. The platform's "Duplication Defense" and "ZeroData ID" products signal a focus on enterprise-grade deduplication and privacy-preserving identity, which is particularly relevant for regulated industries handling large citizen or customer bases.

### Best For

Large enterprises and public sector organizations in financial services, telecommunications, hospitality, or government that need multi-modal biometrics (face + voice), physical access control, and contact center authentication alongside digital KYC — all under a single vendor contract. The AWS Marketplace and Google Cloud Platform availability also makes it attractive for organizations already committed to those cloud ecosystems.

### Avoid If

SMBs or startups that need transparent, self-serve pricing and fast onboarding. Veridas is an enterprise-tier vendor with fully custom pricing and no published rates — procurement cycles will be long, and the product complexity exceeds what most sub-500-employee organizations require.

### Integration Complexity

**Low** — REST API, Web SDK, and iOS/Android Mobile SDKs are all available. Cloud deployment is supported on both AWS Marketplace and Google Cloud Platform, with on-premises options for enterprises with strict data sovereignty requirements. The multi-product platform (facial, voice, access) may require a more involved implementation scoping phase despite the API's technical accessibility.

### Pricing Analysis

Veridas operates exclusively on custom/enterprise pricing with no published tiers. Given the $1B+ funding and acquired status, pricing will reflect enterprise contract expectations — typically annual commitments with volume minimums. Buyers should expect a multi-week procurement process with a dedicated account manager.

For organizations evaluating build-vs-buy for contact center voice biometrics or stadium access control alongside digital KYC, the multi-modal consolidation may justify the premium versus assembling point solutions. Ask specifically about module pricing (face, voice, access separately) versus platform bundle discounts during negotiations.

## FAQ

**Is CORE Identity Verification Solutions the same company as Veridas?**
Yes. CORE Identity Verification Solutions is Veridas's G2 product listing. The underlying company is Veridas, headquartered in Spain, which provides the technology under the Veridas brand across its product portfolio.

**Does Veridas offer voice biometrics for call center authentication?**
Yes. Voice Shield and Voice Authentication are distinct Veridas products designed for contact center and BPO authentication use cases, making it one of the few identity verification vendors that natively covers both digital (web/mobile) and telephony channels.

**What is the Duplication Defense product?**
Duplication Defense is Veridas's deduplication tool designed to identify when the same individual attempts to enroll multiple identities in a system — particularly relevant for government ID programs, loyalty schemes, and regulated financial onboarding where duplicate accounts indicate fraud.

**What does "ZeroData ID" mean in the Veridas product portfolio?**
ZeroData ID appears to be Veridas's privacy-preserving identity wallet concept, designed to allow users to prove identity attributes without transmitting raw biometric data. It aligns with European digital identity regulation (eIDAS 2.0) priorities.

**With zero published reviews on G2, how mature is this product?**
This is an early-stage vendor profile on G2 with no published reviews. Veridas as a company is established (founded 2019, $1B+ funding), but prospective buyers should seek references directly from Veridas rather than relying on third-party review platforms for validation.
