## PrimeBiometry Assessment

1Kosmos differentiates on architecture: it is one of the few identity platforms that combines biometric authentication, identity proofing, and decentralized identity (W3C DID standard) in a single platform, anchoring credentials to a private blockchain to eliminate centralized credential stores as an attack surface. This is a meaningful technical distinction for enterprise security teams concerned with credential theft and phishing. Founded in 2018 and holding a 4.8 Gartner rating (albeit from a small base of 15 reviews), 1Kosmos primarily targets workforce identity (CIAM and workforce IAM) rather than customer KYC pipelines.

### Best For

Mid-market and enterprise organizations replacing legacy password infrastructure with phishing-resistant biometric MFA — particularly those with compliance mandates (NIST AAL2/AAL3) that require both high-assurance authentication and auditable identity proofing for workforce or customer accounts.

### Avoid If

Companies whose primary need is high-volume automated document KYC for consumer onboarding. 1Kosmos is built for identity assurance and authentication continuity, not commodity ID verification pipelines.

## FAQ

**How does 1Kosmos use blockchain for identity?**
1Kosmos anchors identity attributes (document scans, biometric templates, verified credentials) to a private, permissioned blockchain. This means no central credential database exists that can be breached — each user's identity data is distributed and verifiable without a centralized store.

**Does 1Kosmos replace existing IAM systems like Okta or Azure AD?**
1Kosmos is designed to integrate with, not replace, existing IAM platforms. It adds biometric identity proofing and phishing-resistant authentication as a layer that works alongside Okta, Azure AD, and similar systems via SAML/OIDC/SCIM.

**What is BlockID and how does it relate to the 1Kosmos Platform?**
BlockID is 1Kosmos's branded authentication product within the broader 1Kosmos Platform. It handles the biometric authentication and decentralized credential storage components of the platform.

**Is 1Kosmos suitable for NIST AAL2 and AAL3 compliance?**
Yes. 1Kosmos is designed to meet NIST SP 800-63-3 authenticator assurance levels, which is relevant for US federal contractors, healthcare organizations, and any enterprise with formal identity assurance requirements.

**What biometrics does 1Kosmos support?**
1Kosmos supports facial biometrics, fingerprint (on supported devices), and device biometrics (Face ID, Touch ID, Windows Hello) — enabling passwordless login anchored to a verified identity rather than just a device.
