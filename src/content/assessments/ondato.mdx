## PrimeBiometry Assessment

Ondato stands out in the European IDV market for two reasons: its 99.8% accuracy claim (the highest published figure among comparable platforms) and its Virtual Branch product, which enables video-based remote client interaction for regulated onboarding — useful for high-risk customer segments that require human-in-the-loop verification. Headquartered in Lithuania and operating across 192 countries, Ondato's compliance stack is built around EU regulatory frameworks (GDPR, HIPAA, PCI DSS, ISO standards), making it a credible choice for European financial services and gambling platforms where local regulatory familiarity matters. Its $26.4M in private equity funding reflects institutional validation without the valuation pressure of VC-backed competitors.

### Best For

EU-based financial services, insurance, telecom, and online gambling operators that need a full compliance stack — KYC, KYB, AML, transaction monitoring (KYT), and ongoing due diligence — from a single vendor with EU data sovereignty and experience navigating AMLD/GDPR requirements. The Virtual Branch product is a specific fit for banks and legal firms with regulated video-based onboarding requirements.

### Avoid If

Organizations headquartered in North America that prioritize CCPA compliance, US-based support, or vendors with significant US reference customer bases. Ondato's Lithuania base means EU time zones for support and a customer portfolio concentrated in European markets. CCPA certification is not in their published compliance list.

**Low** — REST API, Mobile SDK (iOS/Android), and Web SDK are available with both cloud and on-premise deployment options. Cloud and on-premise configurations support regulated customers who require local data processing. Setup documentation is standard for the IDV market.

### Pricing Analysis

Ondato's published pricing shows a range from $10–49/month for basic tiers up to custom Enterprise pricing, but these figures appear to be placeholder or legacy pricing rather than current active tiers. The production pricing for any serious deployment will require direct sales engagement, particularly for AML screening, transaction monitoring, and Virtual Branch features which are not covered by basic monthly plans.

For EU companies, Ondato's value proposition is strongest when bundling KYC + KYT (transaction monitoring) into a single contract — avoiding the complexity of managing separate IDV and transaction monitoring vendors. Buyers should request specific pricing for the Ondato OS (their integrated compliance operating system) rather than individual point products, as the bundle economics typically improve significantly over à la carte pricing. Companies in the gambling or adult content verticals should ask specifically about regulatory-compliant workflows for age verification and ongoing monitoring.

## FAQ

**What is Ondato's Virtual Branch product?**
Virtual Branch enables regulated video-based remote onboarding and client interaction, allowing compliance officers or agents to conduct identity verification via video call. This is relevant for banks, legal firms, and financial advisors where face-to-face identity verification is required by regulation but in-person meetings are impractical.

**What is KYT (Know Your Transaction) and does Ondato support it?**
Ondato's Transaction Monitoring (KYT) product provides ongoing monitoring of financial transactions for AML compliance, flagging suspicious patterns in real time. This capability is a differentiator versus IDV-only vendors and is particularly valuable for payment platforms and crypto exchanges with ongoing AML monitoring obligations.

**How does Ondato's 99.8% accuracy figure compare to competitors?**
Ondato claims 99.8% accuracy across 192 countries — higher than Veriff's published 99.6% decision rate and Sumsub's 99.5% figure. These metrics are not independently audited in the same way, so buyers should request methodology documentation to compare like-for-like. Ondato holds ISO Level 1 and Level 2 certifications for PAD (liveness detection).

**Does Ondato support business verification (KYB) in addition to individual KYC?**
Yes. Business onboarding is a core product, covering company registry verification, UBO identification, and ongoing due diligence for legal entities. This makes Ondato viable for B2B onboarding in financial services where both individual and business verification are required.

**Is Ondato suitable for age verification in online gambling and adult content platforms?**
Yes. Ondato explicitly lists gambling, adult content, and shared economy as target industries, and its age verification product is designed for these regulated environments. The platform's HIPAA certification also supports healthcare-adjacent use cases where age and identity verification intersect with medical data handling.
