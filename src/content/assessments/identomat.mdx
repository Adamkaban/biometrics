## PrimeBiometry Assessment

Identomat is a Georgian-headquartered identity verification platform that competes on breadth and certification depth: it holds iBeta Level 2 and ISO 30107-3 anti-spoofing certifications alongside SOC 2 Type II and GDPR compliance, covering the full KYC/AML stack from document OCR and face match to Video KYC and AML monitoring in one API. The platform's freemium entry point ($0.45/verification for the Essentials tier covering 350 verifications) targets early-stage fintechs that need regulated-grade liveness without an upfront commitment. With 193+ countries supported and a 40-second average verification time and 98% conversion rate, Identomat positions itself as a cost-efficient alternative to Veriff or Sumsub for SMB buyers.

### Best For

Fintech startups and crypto platforms in the 500-5,000 monthly verifications range that need certified liveness detection (iBeta Level 2), an AML screening add-on, and white-label UI customization without an enterprise contract.

### Avoid If

High-volume operations (50,000+ verifications/month) where per-verification pricing at $0.28-$0.45 significantly outpaces flat-rate competitors. Also avoid if you require on-premises deployment without a custom contract, since self-hosted is only available on the Custom tier.

### Pricing Analysis

Identomat's three-tier model (Essentials at $0.45/verification for 350 verifications, Advanced at $0.28/verification for 1,200 verifications, Custom for bespoke volume) suits startups and growth-stage companies better than enterprises. The Essentials tier's per-verification pricing is transparent but becomes expensive past ~3,000 verifications/month compared to competitors offering volume bundles. The Advanced tier at $0.28/verification is competitive for mid-market buyers in the 1,000-10,000 range.

Custom pricing unlocks on-premises deployment, full white-labeling, custom document OCR, and flexible data residency — the negotiating leverage is volume commitment. Buyers processing 10,000+ monthly verifications should negotiate directly for a volume bundle rather than staying on Advanced.

## FAQ

**Does Identomat support Video KYC for regulated banking use cases?**
Yes. Video KYC is available as an add-on on the Advanced tier and is included in the Custom plan. It supports agent-assisted live video sessions with document review and biometric capture in one workflow.

**What anti-spoofing certifications does Identomat hold?**
Identomat holds iBeta Level 2 PAD (Presentation Attack Detection) certification and conforms to ISO 30107-3, making it suitable for regulated financial institutions with specific liveness certification requirements.

**Can Identomat be deployed on-premises for data residency requirements?**
On-premises deployment is available only on the Custom tier. Buyers with strict data residency requirements (e.g., specific EU member-state hosting) should negotiate this upfront, as it is not included in self-service tiers.

**How does Identomat handle AML screening alongside KYC?**
AML monitoring is available as a module on the Advanced tier and above. It covers sanctions lists, PEP databases, and adverse media screening — bundled in the same verification workflow rather than requiring a separate vendor integration.

**What happens if my verification volume exceeds the Essentials tier limit of 350?**
Identomat bills per-verification overages above the included volume. For predictable scaling, upgrading to Advanced (1,200 included verifications) or negotiating a Custom volume block is more cost-effective than relying on overages.
