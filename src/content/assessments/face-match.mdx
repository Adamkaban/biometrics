## PrimeBiometry Assessment

Face Match by Sightcorp is an SDK-first offering aimed at developers who need to embed face identification directly into their own applications, rather than consuming a managed cloud service. Its liveness detection capability (Liveness Detector) is built into the kit, reducing the integration burden for teams building access control or KYC flows. As an early-stage vendor (founded 2024) with no published reviews, buyers should treat procurement as a pilot engagement and validate accuracy against their own use-case data.

### Best For

Development teams at access control vendors, physical security integrators, or compliance-driven startups who need a portable face recognition SDK to embed in custom applications. The on-premise deployment model suits organizations with data residency requirements that preclude cloud biometrics.

### Avoid If

Organizations looking for a managed identity verification service with a no-code dashboard and SLA-backed uptime. Face Match is an SDK toolkit — without engineering resources to integrate and maintain it, the product delivers no standalone value.

## FAQ

**Is Face Match a cloud service or an on-premise SDK?**
Face Match is an SDK product, meaning it runs on your infrastructure rather than calling an external API. This is appropriate for organizations with strict data residency or latency requirements.

**Does Face Match include liveness detection?**
Yes. The Liveness Detector is included in the SDK to prevent photo and video spoofing attacks. Buyers should test it against their specific threat model — mask attacks and 3D prints warrant particular scrutiny.

**What use cases does Face Match support?**
The SDK is documented for access control, KYC compliance, and general biometric identification in videos, images, and real-time camera feeds.

**Does Face Match have any published accuracy benchmarks?**
No NIST FRVT or third-party benchmark results are publicly available as of this assessment. Buyers should request vendor-supplied test results and validate independently on representative data.

**Who is the company behind Face Match?**
Face Match is a product of Sightcorp, headquartered in Amsterdam, Netherlands. The company was founded in 2024 and has no published reviews on major software review platforms.
