## PrimeBiometry Assessment

EnQualify's technical premise is "AI on Mobile" -- performing identity verification on-device in under 5 seconds rather than relying on server-side processing. This architecture has genuine privacy and latency advantages: biometric data doesn't leave the user's device for the verification decision, which is meaningful for GDPR Article 5 data minimization compliance in principle. However, EnQualify has no published vendor website, no pricing information, zero published reviews, and limited public documentation. This makes it one of the highest-friction vendors to evaluate in this comparison set. Early-stage vendor with limited published reviews.

### Best For

Mobile application developers and digital identity teams at fintech or government agencies that specifically need on-device liveness + facial recognition to minimize latency and server-side data exposure, and are willing to invest in vendor outreach to surface technical and commercial details.

### Avoid If

## FAQ

**What does "AI on Mobile" mean in EnQualify's context?**
EnQualify processes facial recognition and liveness detection on the end user's mobile device rather than sending biometric data to a cloud server for analysis. This approach reduces verification latency and limits server-side biometric data exposure.

**How fast is EnQualify's identity verification process?**
EnQualify claims verification completion in under 5 seconds, driven by on-device processing eliminating server roundtrip latency. This claim has not been independently benchmarked.

**Does EnQualify have a working vendor website?**
As of June 2026, EnQualify does not have a publicly accessible vendor website listed in major review platforms. Procurement teams should reach out via their Capterra listing or direct outreach channels.

**Is on-device biometric processing more GDPR-compliant than cloud processing?**
On-device processing can support GDPR Article 5 data minimization principles by avoiding transmission of biometric data to third-party servers. However, GDPR compliance depends on the full data lifecycle, including enrollment data storage and audit log management -- verify these specifics with the vendor.

**How does EnQualify compare to cloud-based IDV vendors on accuracy?**
On-device AI models typically trade some accuracy against the latency and privacy benefits of local processing. Request accuracy benchmarks (true acceptance rate, false acceptance rate) for your target device range and demographic population before committing.
